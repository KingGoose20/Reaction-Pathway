Question Examples:

Q: Which functional group undergoes Addition to become a Alkane?

A: Alkene



Q: What does a Alkene become under Hydration?

A: Alcohol



Q: What catalyst is needed for a Alkene to undergo Hydration?

A: H3PO4 (Phosphoric Acid)



Q: What are the conditions for an Carboxylic Acid to undergo Condensation?

A: Alkene



Q: What reactant is required for a Alkane to form a Haloalkane through Substitution?

A: X2



Q: What is the product when Carboxylic Acid and Amine undergo Condensation?

A: Amide



Q: Write all the details (reaction mechanism, catalyst, conditions, and reactant) for a Haloalkane to become a Amine?

A: Substitution, Ethanol, RTP, NH3 Gas
